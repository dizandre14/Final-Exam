About Me

Andre Thomas is a young upcoming entrepreneur who's idea of creating a safe place
to sip and enjoy coffee finally became a reality. Through years of hard work, dedication,
and heavy sacrifices, Andre was finally able to achieve his dream. And as CEO of this company his
main motto, as he puts it, is "Never get complacent", because in doing so would be a diservice to not only
you the reader, but also every other customer who visits this shop. And with that said, please feel to read
on and discover more about BeanHaven.

Growing Community

According to the NCA, 64% of Americans consume coffee daily...so you know what that means? Expansion.
we plan on opening more than 100 shops worldwide in the coming months, so if you would like to
help please enter one of our many coffee shops and ask to speak to a representative.

Our Commitment

When BeanHaven was created it was meant to bring everyone together, so
the best way to do that? By giving back. For every store built BeanHaven will donate
a small portion of our yearly earnings of your choice, for more information please
get in touch with any of our shops and ask for an representative.
